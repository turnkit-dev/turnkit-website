Turn Relay

Built for the hard parts of
turn-based multiplayer.

The parts that take weeks to get right — TurnKit handles them out of the box.

⟳

Turn Enforcement

Prevent players from acting out of turn. Server-side validation — no client-side tricks can bypass it.

◑

Hand Hiding

Each player only receives the data they are allowed to see. Cards, stats, hidden moves — all filtered server-side.

✓

Player Voting

Players vote based on your game rules. Validate moves before continuing, or let players dispute outcomes.

⬡

Signed Match Results

Tamper-proof match result generated automatically at match end. Cryptographically signed by the relay.

Every option is configurable per game.
